# Summary

The BISD Framework is a bilingual STEM instructional framework designed for K-5 Two-Way Immersion (TWI) programs. The framework integrates scientific inquiry, collaborative digital learning, laboratory-free experiential science, and bilingual academic language development aligned with NGSS principles.

# Statement of Need

K-5 bilingual STEM programs frequently lack structured Spanish-first science instructional frameworks aligned with NGSS and designed specifically for Two-Way Immersion environments. Existing resources are commonly translations of English curricula rather than frameworks designed directly for bilingual contexts.

The BISD Framework addresses this gap through five integrated components:

- IBICI (Bilingual Scientific Inquiry Cycle)
- MACE (Experiential Scientific Learning Framework)
- TIDE (Technology for Interaction and Team Development)
- BISD Guide
- FRAME dissemination model

The framework was developed through practitioner-researcher action research conducted in a rural North Carolina Two-Way Immersion program.
